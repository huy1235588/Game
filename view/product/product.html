Sekiro™: Shadows Die Twice

Tags

Soul-like Open World Dark Fantasy RPG

ABOUT THIS GAME

Rise, Tarnished, and be guided by grace to brandish the power of the Elden Ring.

• A Breathtaking World Full of Excitement and Mystery

The Lands Between are part of a vast continent where magnificent open fields and huge dungeons with complex and three-dimensional designs are seamlessly connected. As you explore, the joy of discovering unknown and overwhelming threats awaits you. Mastery of the terrain and knowledge of its secrets can help you overcome enemies and defeat formidable bosses or lead invading players into traps.

• Defeat Challenging Foes in Intense Combat

Combat in ELDEN RING is simple to learn yet offers hidden depths of mastery. As you seek to become the Elden Lord, you’ll need to explore the balance between attacking and avoiding damage, use a wide variety of weapons, spells, & summons, and perfect your timing to take advantage of your opponents’ weaknesses.

• Create and Build Your Own Character

In addition to customizing the appearance of your character, there are countless ways to combine the weapons, armor, usable items, and magic that you equip. You can develop your character according to your play style. No matter if you prefer bold physical confrontation, tactical spellcasting, or the subtle art of stealth, you’ll be able to find gear that supports your choices.
